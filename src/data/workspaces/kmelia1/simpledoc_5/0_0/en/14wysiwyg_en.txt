<p>The back office is only accessible to general administrators and, in part, to space administrators. Administration of SILVERPEAS does not require technical knowledge. It&#39;s accessible to non-IT users thanks to a simple and ergonomic interface, consistent with that of the front office. Each page has breadcrumbs and the menu : What do you want to do?</p>
