<h3 class="wysiwyg-title2">Presentation</h3>

<p>The Classification Plan is a multi-dimensional classification system composed of several axes.<br />
Each axis carries a strong notion of the company (Professions, Products, Themes, Geographic, Document typology, etc.) which will be broken down using a tree of values. These axes and their sub axis are created freely from the platform&#39;s back office by the administrator or by delegation.</p>

<p>Depending on the nature and importance of the information, contributors will be able to classify it on the appropriate axes and values ​​of the Classification Plan.<br />
A position is made up of a set of axes/values. The same contribution can have several positions on the Classification Plan.</p>
